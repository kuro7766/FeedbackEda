[Claim,Effective] A student-designed project allows a student to show their creativity because they're making it their own. Also students being able to express their creativity allows each student to be their own. This benefits the school and the students in it for it could create bonds between students that share the same likes and dislikes, let the teacher and adults get to know more about the students, and helps shape the diversity within the school.
