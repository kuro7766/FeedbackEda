First of all, classrooms have a live, and trained teacher. Students are able to learn from someone that they get to know throughout the year. With a live teacher, the learner can ask and answer questions easily and receive personal attention concerning their individual issues with topics or assignments. Having classes aligned for the week can enable students to practice time management and help them be alert for appropriate times throughout each day.
